Teacher Help

Welcome to the Teacher Help page. Here you can find answers to common questions and resources to assist you in managing exams and students.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I create a new exam?
    Go to the "Exams" section, click "Create Exam", fill in the required details, and save your exam.
  • How can I view student submissions?
    Navigate to the "Submissions" page, select the relevant exam, and review student responses.
  • How do I grade exams?
    Open the exam submissions, review each student's answers, and assign grades using the grading interface.
  • Who do I contact for technical support?
    Please email support@example.com for assistance.
